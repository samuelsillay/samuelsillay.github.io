Fixed geared Bikes/Fixies

Fixed gear bikes or "fixies" are single speed mostly road bikes. They are usually a blend of road bike style frames but with a more upright position with wide riser bars. What makes them the most different from a road bike is the fact that you are unable to coast since there is no free-wheel in the rear hub. This makes it more difficult for the rider if there are steep hills since you only have one gear but it leaves the rider with a connected feeling to his/her bike.
