The World of Bikes as seen by Sam Sillay

The mountain bike pictured above is my Felt Decree. It is a trail/enduro bike designed to have a soft ride on both agressive and chunky terrain but still efficient enough to still pedal well. I have raced this bike in all sorts of events and it did suprisingly well in most categories. I used it in multiple cross country races in the summer, and pushed it hard for Umaine's team this past fall in dual slalom, enduro and downhill.
